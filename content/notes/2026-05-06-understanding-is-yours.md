你可以外包你的思考，但你不能外包你的理解。

在 vibe coding 的时代，写代码本身不再是最难的部分。真正重要的是你是否理解代码背后的逻辑、系统的结构、以及一切到底是怎么运行的。

Claude Code 和 Codex 这样的工具无可否认地强大。它们能帮你更快地构建、更快地调试、更快地探索想法。但如果没有真正的理解，它们很容易变成脱缰的野马：跑得快、看着厉害、却很危险。

在 AI 时代，责任和信任比以往任何时候都更重要。

当你的代码出了问题，你不能简单地甩锅给 AI。上线的是你，承担结果的也是你。

所以目标永远不应该是："我只需要学会怎么用 AI 写代码就行了。"

真正的目标是：你需要理解 AI 写了什么、为什么这么写、以及它可能在哪里出错。

你可以让 AI 加速你的思考。

但理解必须是你自己的。

因为只有理解才能让你真正负责。

<!-- LANG:EN -->

You can outsource your thinking, but you cannot outsource your understanding.

In the age of vibe coding, writing code itself is no longer the hardest part. What truly matters is whether you understand the logic behind the code, the structure of the system, and how everything actually runs.

Tools like Claude Code and Codex are undeniably powerful. They can help you build faster, debug faster, and explore ideas faster. But without real understanding, they can easily become runaway horses: fast, impressive, and dangerous.

In the AI era, responsibility and trust matter more than ever.

When your code breaks, you cannot simply blame AI. You are still the one who ships it. You are still the one who owns the outcome.

So the goal should never be: "I only need to know how to use AI to write code."

The real goal is this: you need to understand what AI writes, why it writes it that way, and where it might go wrong.

You can let AI accelerate your thinking.

But your understanding must remain your own.

Because only understanding makes you responsible.
